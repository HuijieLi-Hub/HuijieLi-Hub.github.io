Huijie Li

I am a biomedical engineer specializing in the development of advanced biochemical sensors and integrated dashboards for healthcare applications. I have expertise in CRISPR-based diagnostics, aptamer-enabled biosensing, and wearable sensor platforms, bridging nanomaterials engineering, microfabrication, and electrochemical detection. I am particularly interested in creating real-time, multiplexed monitoring systems that transform complex biochemical data into actionable insights, enabling personalized and preventative healthcare.
